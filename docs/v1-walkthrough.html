Policy Overview

For billing disputes over $500, agents follow the tiered approach below. Based on: Refund Auth Limits + CX lead input

Resolution Tiers

Tier 1: $500–$1,000

  • Full refund authorized per CX lead
  • 15% goodwill discount per Retention Guidelines
  • 3–5 business day timeline

Tier 2: $1,000–$2,500

  • Agent refund up to $1,000; supervisor above

Tier 3: Over $2,500

  • Escalate with $500 interim credit
  • 48hr SLA per Q1 Dispute SOP
